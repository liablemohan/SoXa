ABOUT US

INTRODUCTION

SoXa Cultural Institute is dedicated to advancing the understanding of cultural traditions from around the world. We support researchers, educators, and institutions focused on comparative cultural studies, anthropology, folklore, and digital humanities through intellectual resources, collaborative networks, and funding.

We connect donors with meaningful cultural research and preservation projects across diverse global communities.

OUR MISSION

In an increasingly interconnected world, cultural knowledge systems face unprecedented challenges. SoXa Cultural Institute seeks to document, preserve, and share diverse cultural traditions through innovative research methodologies, digital archiving, and international collaboration.

We direct grants and scholarships to researchers and institutions conducting meaningful work in cultural preservation, language documentation, traditional arts, and heritage studies across the globe.

LEADERSHIP

Dr. Alexandra Chen

Founder & Director

Dr. Chen is a renowned cultural anthropologist specializing in comparative mythology and digital humanities. She holds a Ph.D. from Stanford University and has conducted extensive fieldwork across Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

Her groundbreaking research on cultural transmission in the digital age has been published in leading academic journals and has shaped contemporary approaches to heritage preservation.
