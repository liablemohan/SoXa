ℹ️ Our Story

About the Platform

SoXa is a Sanskrit-focused, community-driven, open-access scholarly hub. Every design decision — from our colour palette to our licensing model — is made to serve the contributor and reader, not the institution.

Our Identity

Three Repositories. One Platform. One Philosophy.

SoXa is the only open-access platform to combine a Thesis Repository, an Epigraphical Archive, and a Literary History database under a unified design system. No login required. No paywalls. Full citation export, Devanāgarī rendering, and EPUB/PDF downloads for everything.

Our founding philosophy, as stated by the founder: "Contributors write for themselves. The platform is merely the medium of amplification." Every UX decision flows from this principle.

Audience Design

Two Audiences, One Coherent Platform

Both scholar and general reader are first-class citizens. Neither is forced to navigate through the other's experience.

🎓

Scholar / Researcher

Full repository access with citation export (APA / Chicago / MLA / BibTeX / TEI-XML), IAST + Devanāgarī script toggle, advanced search with faceted filters, thesis metadata, epigraphic diplomatic transcription, and TEI-XML downloads. Motivation: citation, credibility, speed to relevant material.

📖

General Reader / Student

Curated highlights, 'start here' reading paths, plain-language introductions to each repository, newsletter, event calendar, and community stories. Motivation: discovery, accessibility, community belonging. Reading mode (Light / Sepia / Dark) available at all times.

Our Journey

Platform Milestones

2023

Platform Conception

The founding brief is drafted following a review of Sanskrit digital humanities infrastructure. The gap: no platform combines a thesis repository, epigraphic database, and literary history under a unified open-access design.

2024

Competitive Audit & Design System

Global landscape survey of 50 Sanskrit institutions and a detailed audit of 6 high-similarity websites (Tsadra Foundation, 84000, Lotsawa House, CIF, Rocher Foundation, Mangalam Research Center) informs the Design System v1.0.

2025

Repository Seeding & First Contributors

First 87 scholarly contributors onboarded. Epigraphical Archive reaches 1,000 entries. Thesis Repository accepts first international submissions. Partnership with BORI, Pune and ORI-MSU, Baroda announced.

2026

Phase 1 Launch

The platform officially launches with all three repositories, reading mode (Light / Sepia / Dark), script toggle (Devanāgarī / IAST / Roman), mobile-responsive design, and WCAG AA accessibility compliance throughout.

Design Philosophy

What Makes SoXa Different

🔤

Devanāgarī-First Rendering

All Sanskrit text renders in Shobhika or Noto Devanāgarī at ≥20px. IAST transliteration uses Source Serif 4 for correct diacritic rendering. Zero benchmarked competitors offer this.

📖

Reading Comfort by Specification

Article body: 17px, line-height 1.85, max column 680px. Background #FDFCF9. Contrast ratio ≥7:1 (WCAG AAA). Dark + sepia reading modes. No competitor offers all of these together.

♿

Accessibility as a Launch Requirement

WCAG AA throughout; AAA for article body text. All animations wrapped in prefers-reduced-motion. Keyboard-navigable throughout. Focus indicators on every interactive element.
