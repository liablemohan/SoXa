ABOUT US

INTRODUCTION

Tattva Heritage Foundation is dedicated to the preservation and promotion of Indian culture and heritage. We support scholars, artists and institutions focused on Sanskrit language, literature, religious-cultural traditions, and intellectual resources, networks and funding.

We help potential donors channel their philanthropy into cultural and heritage projects.

OUR MISSION

Yesterday's cultural inheritance, the repository of patronage, much of India's cultural heritage, both tangible and intangible, has come under immense pressure. Tattva Heritage Foundation seeks to help persons through cultural institutions building formulating ways to channel resources for religious-cultural activities, and by directing scholarships and grants to selected scholars, through art and cultural institutions committed to excellence.

OUR VISION

Tattva Heritage Foundation aims to revive the patronage network that for millennia supported the custodians, practitioners, philosophers, theologians, grammarians, logicians, poets, saints and so much more. We believe that culture is harnessed authentically is extremely empowering as it provides a sense of identity, belonging and harmony. We envision collaborations that create a network rooted to our own, ancestral land and community.

KEY PERSON

Manish Maheshwari

Manish Maheshwari is the founder of the Tattva Heritage Foundation. His interests span Indian intellectual traditions, and the institutional conditions necessary for the instituted study and transmission of cultural knowledge. Prior to establishing the Foundation, he worked in investment management in New York and Mumbai. He holds postgraduate degrees from Columbia University and Mumbai University.
