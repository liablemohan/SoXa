    Ways to Contribute

    Find Your Role

    Four distinct pathways — choose one or combine multiple based on your expertise, time, and interests.

    🔍

    Peer Reviewer

    ⏱ 4–8 hrs / month

    Evaluate submitted theses, epigraphical records, and literary texts for scholarly accuracy, metadata completeness, and research quality within your domain.

    • Assigned to your subject panel (Literature, Epigraphy, or Linguistics & Philosophy)
    • Structured review form with standardized rubric
    • Named credit on all reviewed records
    • Annual reviewer certificate & recognition on community page
    • First access to new platform features
    Apply as Reviewer →
    🎓

    Campus Ambassador

    ⏱ 2–4 hrs / month

    Represent SoXa at your institution. Spread awareness, encourage thesis submissions, coordinate outreach events, and serve as the local point of contact.

    • Official SoXa Ambassador letter for your department
    • Social media toolkit & promotional materials
    • Event hosting grants for on-campus SoXa workshops
    • Mentorship from the community team
    • Certificate & LinkedIn recommendation on completion
    Apply as Ambassador →
    ✍️

    Content Contributor

    ⏱ Flexible

    Write for the SoXa blog, newsletter, or annual journal. Topics include Sanskrit research findings, digital humanities tools, reviews of recent publications, and more.

    • Published under CC BY 4.0 with full authorship credit
    • Editorial support and co-editing for journal articles
    • Platform-wide visibility across 38+ institutions
    • Peer review of your submitted work
    • Listed in the annual contributor digest
    Submit Your Work →
    🗂️

    Data Volunteer

    ⏱ 1–3 hrs / sprint

    Participate in short-format sprint projects: geo-tagging inscriptions, metre tagging ślokas, transcribing signboard text, or cataloguing regional manuscripts.

    • Micro-sprint format — contribute in a single afternoon
    • No advanced expertise required for most tasks
    • Contribution count displayed on your public profile
    • Gamified achievements & milestone badges
    • SoXa volunteer certificate after 5 completed sprints
    Browse Sprint Projects →
