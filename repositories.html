Thesis Repository

Doctoral and postgraduate theses in Sanskrit studies, Indology, and related disciplines. Fully searchable by author, university, year, and subject. Each entry includes a structured abstract, citation, and open-access PDF/EPUB download.

240+ theses · Open Access · EPUB + PDF
🪨

Epigraphical Archive

Ancient inscriptions from across the Indian subcontinent — copper plates, stone pillars, cave inscriptions — with diplomatic transcriptions, translations, script classification, and location data. Downloadable as TEI-XML.

1,200+ inscriptions · TEI-XML · Location data
📚

Literary History

A community-authored, encyclopaedic history of Sanskrit literature from the Vedic period to the 21st century. Articles are peer-reviewed, cross-linked, and open to scholarly contribution.

80+ articles · Community-authored · Peer-reviewed
