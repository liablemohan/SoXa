Our Mission

We are committed to preserving, promoting, and advancing the study of India's cultural heritage through research, documentation, and education.

Research Excellence

Supporting scholars, artists, and institutions in their pursuit of excellence through rigorous research and intellectual resources.

Cultural Preservation

Documenting and safeguarding India's intangible cultural traditions and traditional knowledge systems for future generations.

Academic Collaboration

Building networks of scholars and institutions committed to advancing the understanding of Indian traditions.
