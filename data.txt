 1. ORIGINAL CLIENT REQUIREMENTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The client wants to build a website focused on Sanskrit higher education, with
an emphasis on modern, skill-based approaches rather than traditional methods.
The goal is visibility and recognition for both Sanskrit scholarship and the
organization itself.

Core Pillars:
  - Community-driven contributions: platform is a medium, not a gatekeeper.
  - Skill-based focus over conventional academic publishing models.
  - Recognition & visibility for contributors and the organization.
  - Maximum student engagement across institutions in India and internationally.
